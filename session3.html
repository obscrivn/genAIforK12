Session 3: Designing AI-Enhanced Assignments

Overview

This session is dedicated to empowering educators to design assignments that integrate AI effectively. Participants will apply principles of AI integration and utilize available resources to create assignments tailored to their subject areas.

Key Resources

  • AI Integration Principles: Guide to Integrating Generative AI
  • Resource Hub: Our Resource Hub

Activity Instructions

Follow these steps to create your AI-enhanced assignments:

  • Form teams based on subject areas (e.g., science, art, history).
  • Collaborate to brainstorm ideas for assignments where AI can add value (e.g., generating creative prompts, analyzing data, or simulating scenarios).
  • Draft your assignment, including clear objectives, expected outcomes, and any necessary instructions for students.
  • Utilize the resources provided to explore AI tools and integrate them into your assignment design.

Presentation

Each team will present their AI-enhanced assignments to the group:

  • Explain the purpose and objectives of your assignment.
  • Demonstrate how AI tools are integrated.
  • Invite feedback and suggestions from other participants.

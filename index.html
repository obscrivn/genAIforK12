About the Workshop

This workshop is a part of Educational Outreach Program. For more information and workshop schedules, see Prism at Rose.


This workshop equips K-12 educators with the knowledge, skills, and resources to integrate AI effectively into classrooms. Participants will gain a comprehensive understanding of AI and its applications.

  • What: Understand AI fundamentals
  • How: Experiment with AI-enabled tools
  • Why: Create text, images, and other teaching materials
  • Where: Apply AI in classrooms for educators and students
  • Perils: Address biases, challenges, and ethical concerns
